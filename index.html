Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Delulu Kart ("the app", "we", "us") is a browsing app for exploring a product catalogue. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

Summary

  • No sign-up or login is required. You can browse the app anonymously.
  • We do not process real payments. Checkout is entirely simulated. No payment information is collected, transmitted, or stored.
  • Your wishlist, cart, browsing activity, and preferences remain on your device and are not copied to our servers.
  • If you submit a product review, that review is stored on our backend and is publicly visible inside the app.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not require or collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Passwords or account credentials
  • Payment information of any kind
  • Precise location data

The checkout experience is purely a demonstration. It does not connect to any payment gateway, bank, card processor, or UPI service.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Your wishlist, cart contents, browsing history within the app, and preferences are stored only on your own device.

This information is never transmitted to us and is automatically removed if you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

Public Content You Submit (Reviews)

If you write a product review, that review is stored on our backend and displayed publicly to other users.

Please do not include personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, or location in a review, as it will be visible to anyone using the app.

You are not required to provide your identity in order to submit a review.

Dummy Checkout

The checkout experience is completely fictional and exists only for demonstration purposes.

No order is placed, no payment is processed, and no payment information (including card numbers, UPI IDs, billing addresses, or similar information) is collected, transmitted, or stored.

Automatically Collected Technical Data

Like most modern apps, Delulu Kart relies on standard platform and hosting services that may automatically generate technical logs required for normal operation.

Examples may include:

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Error logs

We do not use this information to personally identify you, nor do we sell or share it for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Content

Product images displayed inside the app are served through third-party content delivery services for demonstration purposes.

Viewing these images may result in standard internet requests between your device and those services. We do not control or receive personal information from those providers beyond what is necessary to display the images.

Children's Privacy

Delulu Kart is not directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves—for example, if we introduce user accounts, real payments, analytics, or additional services.

Whenever changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
